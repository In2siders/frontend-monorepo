## Common issues

### I cannot log in

1. Confirm your username and password.
2. Make sure Caps Lock is disabled.
3. Retry from a private/incognito browser tab.
4. If the issue continues, contact support with your username.

### I cannot create a chat

- Verify your internet connection.
- Refresh the page and try again.
- If this persists, report the exact error message to support.

### Messages do not appear

- Reload the page.
- Re-open the chat room.
- If needed, sign out and sign in again.

## Security reminders

- Never share your password.
- Do not reuse passwords from other websites.
- Use a strong and unique password for your account.
